Hamburger Menu Centering & Click-Outside Test

This test will verify that the hamburger menu is properly centered on small screens and that clicking outside the menu closes it without triggering other actions.

Open Pro-G App

Test Checklist

  • Menu centers properly on mobile screens (phone sizes)
  • Menu centers properly on tablet screens
  • Clicking outside the menu closes it
  • First click outside does NOT trigger other elements
  • Backdrop covers entire screen on mobile
  • Menu has subtle backdrop blur effect
  • Clicking on menu items still works normally
  • Escape key closes the menu
